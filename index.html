Why Tactiq Works
Built on proven coaching principles
🏆
UEFA Methodology
Every session follows the same four-phase structure used by UEFA-licensed coaches — Activation, Technical, Game Scenario and Cool Down. Not random drills. Structured player development.
⚡
Built in Under 60 Seconds
Answer 5 questions about your age, ability and focus. Tactiq generates a complete session with pitch diagrams, coaching points and progressions — instantly. No planning. No guesswork.
📐
Visual Pitch Diagrams
Every phase includes a clear SVG pitch diagram showing player positions, movement patterns and equipment placement. Export the whole session as a PDF and take it to the pitch.
