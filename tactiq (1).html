Solo Session · Player Plan
U12 Striker — Individual Finishing
Age: Under 12 45 mins High Intensity Shooting & Finishing
Activation · 7 mins
Strike Mechanics Warm-Up
From 8m: 10 soft inside-foot shots into goal focusing purely on technique — locked ankle, non-kicking foot beside ball, head down through contact. Build confidence before adding pace.
Technical · 18 mins
Four Corner Target Drill
Goal divided into 4 zones: TL, TR, BL, BR. From 16m: 5 shots per zone — inside foot for low targets, laces for upper corners. 30s rest between zones. Track score each set.
Game Scenario · 13 mins
Movement & Finish Under Pressure
Start 20m from goal. Roll ball to the side, accelerate onto it, set and shoot within 3 seconds. Vary approach angle: straight, right channel, left channel. 10 shots total.
Cool Down · 7 mins
Deliberate Penalty Practice
10 slow, deliberate penalty kicks — one target per kick, pure placement over power. Finish with quad and hip flexor stretches.

Solo Session · Player Plan
Home Ball Mastery Session
All Ages 30 mins Medium Intensity Dribbling & Control
Activation · 5 mins
Sole Roll Wake-Up
Toe-taps on ball 60 seconds alternating feet. Sole rolls forward and back, then side to side. Build from slow to match pace over 3 minutes. No equipment needed.
Technical · 12 mins
Inside-Outside Cone Weave
6 household items as markers, 1m apart. Weave using inside and outside of right foot for 3 reps, left foot 3 reps, then alternate. Focus on sharp direction changes, not speed.
Game Scenario · 8 mins
Box Dribble Challenge
Mark a 5m x 5m square. 60-second timer — dribble continuously, changing direction every time you reach a marker. Count touches. Try to beat your score each round.
Cool Down · 5 mins
Free Touch & Stretch
3 minutes slow relaxed dribbling — no rules, just enjoy the feel. Finish with quad and hip flexor stretches.

Team Session · Coach & Club Plan
U15 Team High Press Session
Under 15 60 mins High Intensity Defending & Pressing
Activation · 8 mins
Press Trigger Walkthrough
Walk through 3 press triggers as a team: back pass to GK, player faces own goal, heavy first touch. At each trigger the nearest player presses and the rest compact. Slow and deliberate — nail the shape before adding pace.
Technical · 24 mins
6v6 Shadow Press Drill
6 v 6 shadow play — pressing team presses shape but does not tackle. Team in possession plays out from the back. Pressing team learns press shape and trigger recognition without the chaos of live defending.
Game Scenario · 20 mins
Press to Score — 8v8
8 v 8 full game. When the team wins the ball high up the pitch, they score a bonus goal. Forces maximum intensity in the press and rewards winning the ball in dangerous positions.
Cool Down · 8 mins
Shape Review & Debrief
Walk through pressing shape one final time at walking pace. Team discussion: which press triggers worked best, what broke the press, two things done well.

Team Session · Coach & Club Plan
Matchday Recovery Session
All Ages 30 mins Low Intensity Game Intelligence & Mindset
Activation · 5 mins
Light Jog & Dynamic Mobility
Easy jog around pitch perimeter, reducing to walk. Leg swings forward and lateral, walking lunges with rotation, ankle circles. Heart rate stays low throughout — this is recovery, not fitness.
Technical · 12 mins
Possession Rondo — Low Intensity
6+1 v 2 Rondo in a large grid. Emphasis on ball movement and communication only — no pressing intensity. Players move freely and enjoy the football. Touch count unlimited.
Game Scenario · 8 mins
Match Review Discussion
Team circle. Coach leads open discussion: what went well, one moment each player is proud of, one thing the team will improve next week. Keep it positive and forward-looking.
Cool Down · 5 mins
Full Team Static Stretch
Team stretching together: hamstrings, quads, calves, hip flexors, groin. 25 seconds each. End with a team huddle and one word each describing how they feel going into next week.
